Get and share rides with MoveMates

MoveMates is a simple, private assistant that connects riders with drivers right from WhatsApp. Share your pickup and destination, see matches, and ride with people in your community.

No apps to install. No clutter. Just quick rides, organized safely.

Get started

How it works

  1. Message us on WhatsApp.
  2. Share your pickup, destination, and time.
  3. We surface potential drivers or riders.
  4. Confirm a match and go.

Built for campuses, events, neighborhoods, and commuters.

Why MoveMates

  • Fast: Coordinate rides where you already chat.
  • Private by default: We only use the info needed to match rides.
  • Community-first: Safer matches inside known groups.
  • No installs: Works over WhatsApp—no new app required.

About MoveMates

MoveMates started with a simple idea: ride-sharing should be as easy as sending a message. We help communities organize reliable rides without juggling multiple apps or exposing unnecessary data.

What we value

  • Simplicity: People already use WhatsApp; we meet them there.
  • Privacy: Minimal data, clear choices, and easy deletion.
  • Trust: Real people, transparent coordination, and community guidelines.

Privacy Policy

MoveMates operates a WhatsApp-based ride-sharing assistant. When you communicate with our WhatsApp number, we receive your WhatsApp phone number and the messages you send to us. We use this information only to assist in connecting riders with drivers.

Information We Collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your message content related to ride coordination

How We Use This Information

We use the collected information solely for facilitating ride matching between users. Your data is not shared, sold, or transferred to any third party.

You may request removal of your data from our system at any time.
